Pancakes

Ingredient Amount
Plain flour 100 g
Large eggs 2 pcs
Milk 300 ml
Sunflower oil 1 tbsp
Sugar 25 g
  1. Step 1: Put 100g plain flour, 2 large eggs, 300ml milk and 1 tbsp sunflower oil into a bowl, then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Step 2: Set a frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  3. Step 3: When hot, cook your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden.
  4. Step 4: Serve with your favourite filling.
